Thank you to our sponsors and partners

Partner with us!

Sponsoring the Canadian University Chess Championships means being part of the largest university chess tournament in Canada! In gratitude for your generosity, check out some of the great benefits that we are offering. Please feel free to contact us at any time if you would like to sponsor our event!

Potential benefits for sponsors:

  • Company logo displayed on competition banner
  • Company logo added onto move sheets
  • Dedicated company booth at the CUCC
  • Special mention during opening ceremony.
  • Company logo on website homepage, Facebook group and competition pamphlet
  • Company logo with a description of up to 50 words on club website under partners tab
  • Sponsored company post on our club Facebook Group and Event for the CUCC 2017
  • Acknowledge support of sponsors in email for CUCC participants
  • Acknowledge support of sponsors in email to members of the UWO Chess Club

Looking for other ways to sponsor us?

We are also looking to borrow chess boards and timers! Please get in touch to discuss the potential benefits offered for a loan of chess boards/timers.
