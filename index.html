Bringing Western University Chess and GO Players together

Welcome to the Western Chess & GO Club!

We are home to a group of Chess & GO Players at Western University ranging from beginners to master level players. The club is currently not meeting over the summer, but keep your eyes on this page for new developments once school resumes in September!

We are always looking for new members to join our club! There are many ways to get involved and play chess together with our weekly meetings and social media discussion.

This year, The Western Chess & GO Club is also honoured to host the annual Canadian University Chess Championships (CUCC). Follow the CUCC link for more information!
