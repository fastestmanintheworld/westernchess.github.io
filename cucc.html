CUCC 2017 Information

This year the Canadian University Chess Championships (CUCC) is coming to Western University. This annual tournament features the best university chess teams across Canada locked in a battle to outwit their opponent, lay claim to first place and subsequently, have their names engraved on the Queen's Cup.

Eligibility

  • All tournament members must be university students or staff
  • All tournament members must be CFC members

Tournament Format

  • 5 rounds Team Swiss (Teams of 4 players)
  • Teams are allowed but not required to have a fifth player on their team
  • Teams can sub in the fifth player as they please
  • The board order must be locked before round 1.
  • For team formation, it should respect the order of strength.

Time Control

  • 60 min + 30 sec/move for Round 1
  • 90 min + 30 sec/move for Rounds 2 to 5
